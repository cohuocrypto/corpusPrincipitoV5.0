# Methodology

## Phase 1 — Design
Definition of scope, objective and reproducibility criteria.

## Phase 2 — Collection
Collection of multilingual source texts in EN, ES and FR.

## Phase 3 — Curation and Preprocessing
Unicode normalization, OCR cleaning, chapter segmentation, sentence segmentation and dialogue extraction.

## Phase 4 — Annotation
Automatic annotation of character, speaker, emotion, polarity and subjectivity.

## Phase 5 — Evaluation
Validation through coverage metrics, quality flags and distribution analysis.

## Phase 6 — Publication
Frozen release, manifest, validation report, citation file and reproducible scripts.
